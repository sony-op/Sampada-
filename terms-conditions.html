5. Cancellations & Refunds

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both users and venue partners:

  • 60+ days before event: Full refund of advance payment, minus a 5% processing fee
  • 30–59 days before event: 50% of advance payment refunded
  • 15–29 days before event: 25% of advance payment refunded
  • Less than 15 days: No refund on advance payment; remaining balance waived
  • Venue-initiated cancellations: Full refund plus a 10% compensation credit for future bookings

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method. Specific venue cancellation policies may override this schedule and will be communicated at booking.
